Research

My main interests are the asymptotic spectral analysis of matrices and the low-rank factorization of matrices. I have worked on matrix sequences coming from the discretization of linear PDE, on random matrix polynomial with growing size and/or degree, on perturbation of Hermitian matrices, and on matrices utilized in iterative methods. Moreover, I worked in community detection, clustering and predition problems through the lens of low-rank factorization methods, with a focus in nonnegative matrix factorization.

Supported by the ERC consolidator grant no 101085607 of the European Union (Sep 2023 - Jun 2026)
Project: Beyond Low-Rank Factorizations (eLinoR)
